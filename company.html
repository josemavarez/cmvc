A Tradition of Excellence

Compu-Med Vocational Careers (CMVC) has equipped healthcare professionals with the specialized skills needed to excel in this ever-changing industry for over 30 years. From experienced instructors and multiple locations throughout Hialeah and Miami, Florida - CMVC is committed to providing its students with quality instruction that will enable them to reach their highest potential within the field.

Students' present and future successes

CMVC is dedicated to providing students with the opportunity to hone all of skills necessary for success in today's competitive job market. From affiliations such as the Florida Association of Postsecondary Schools and Colleges (FAPSC) to the National Association for Health Professionals (NAHP), CMVC strives to establish partnerships that will provide its learners with cutting-edge training required to thrive within an ever-evolving workforce. In addition, CMVC proudly holds ties with various prestigious organizations like the Hialeah Chamber of Commerce alongside other esteemed institutions, including The Association of Private Colleges and Schools South Florida (APCS) and the National Association of Dental Assistants (NADA).

Our History of Growth

Compu-Med Vocational Careers has been empowering students to pursue their dreams in the healthcare industry since 1989. From Medical and Dental Training Center, which was our original name, we have grown into an accredited institution offering a wide range of vocational programs such as radiology technician or dental assistant certifications. Our mission is to help people gain skills needed for success in various medical facilities, including hospitals, clinics, and nursing homes so they can make a positive impact on society with this vital knowledge.

Humble but ambitious beginnings

It began in a humble two-classroom facility on Okeechobee Road, just outside Hialeah, Florida. Back then, we had only Medical and Dental Assistant programs to offer students, but our ambition meant that the school was destined for greater things! In April 1993, those dreams became a reality when we moved into a larger building - not only did this mean the introduction of Nursing Assistants (now known as Patient Care Technicians), but it also set us up for further expansion with English as Second Language courses and Computer Business Applications being added over time. Our success was reflected by steadily increasing student numbers; growth necessitated another move to an even bigger place nearby at 2900 W 12th Ave., 3rd Floor, where you'll find us today! The accreditation followed shortly after these moves: firstly granted in 1994 before being renewed again in 1996 – from small beginnings came something extraordinary.

First successes and Education offer expansion

In 1998, the school began its journey to Florida by establishing a branch facility in Miami and offering classes from Medical Assistant, Dental Assistant, to Nursing Assistance. Soon after setting up shop, initial accreditation was received for this location just one year later. Over time our Branch facility saw many changes; in October 2000 we relocated 9738 S.W 24th Street where both schools obtained renewal of their accreditations from 2012 until 2017 following a decision made by the Board of Directors that allowed for enhancement and modification, which were granted official approval come June 2011.
