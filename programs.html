A Partnership in
Healthcare Education

CMVC offers countless learning opportunities provided at its locations in Florida. Inspiring healthcare professionals can access CMVC’s state-of-the-art laboratories and robust classroom environments and gain knowledge through top-notch instructors with quality field experience. High-tech experiences are provided for CMVC students to gain an understanding of how to navigate the modern-day workforce by providing experiences with enhanced audiovisual material. As a school that puts students as a top priority, graduates leave with the gain knowledge and confidence needed to become valuable healthcare members of the Florida workforce. If becoming a healthcare professional is what you desire, allow CMVC to help advise and guide you through your vocational training journey. After all, this is where learning begins and a partnership in healthcare education forms.

Compu-Med Accreditation

Accreditation is a voluntary process in which an institution or agency is evaluated against established criteria to ensure that it meets specific standards. The purpose of accreditation is to provide quality assurance and improve public trust in the institutions and agencies that offer accredited programs in health care.

Compu-Med Healthcare Training programs are Licensed by the Commission for Independent Education, Florida Department of Education, and Accredited by the Accrediting Commission of Careers Schools and Colleges (ACCSC).
